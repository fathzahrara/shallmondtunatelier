Original Character

Name : Lillie Quackenbush
Age : 12 y.o
Birth : 27 October
Trait : logical thinking, deduction
Likes : astronomy, geology, mineral stone, nature, illustrated books, star, loaf, money
Dislike : Unable to find the answer she've been looking for
Associated animals : sckhunkus, squirrel
Colors : dimmes pink, light blue, pinkish grey.
Lillie was 6 years old when her father left her and her mother. Back when she still 4 years old, she attached with his father. Any 'weird' things her father gave her become her treasure afterwards. She didn't possess magic, instead she posses amount of knowledge she wishes to know. Her magical element is terra that's why she loves to explore more about the ground she stepped on . Her mother left her then after remarry some high caste widow and left her behind due to high rules to not letting anyone who arent related to the relative (e.g mom dad) or below the "high caste" original blood itself. She then kicked out from the house when she was 8 years old and start looking for an old tree to make her own "castle" there. The "gear" on her hat is one of the memento her father gave her when she were young, it said that it's from unused town clock gear back when her father help to repair it.
